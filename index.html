Discover Kolkata

Immerse yourself in the lively streets, historic landmarks, and delicious flavors of this cultural gem of India.

Top three activities to do at Kolkata

Stroll through Howrah Bridge

Take a walk across this iconic cantilever bridge that spans the Hooghly River.

Take a Ferry Ride on the Hooghly River

Enjoy a scenic ferry ride to experience the city from the water.

Indulge in Authentic Bengali Cuisine

Visit popular eateries or street food stalls to experience the city's culinary heritage firsthand.

Top three places to visit at Kolkata

Victoria Memorial

A grand marble structure surrounded by lush gardens, housing a museum with art and historical exhibits.

Visit Dakshineswar Kali Temple

This famous temple dedicated to Goddess Kali is located on the banks of the Hooghly River.

Explore the Indian Museum

One of the oldest museums in India, it houses an extensive collection of artifacts, including sculptures, paintings, and fossils.
